Davide's research focuses on video understanding. His recent work includes procedural planning (proposing a sequence of procedural steps given an initial and a goal state), adverb recognition (understanding the manner actions are performed), localised instruction generation (segmenting procedural videos and describing steps with natural language), generalised zero-shot action recognition (testing models on unseen classes). Currently he is now working on interpretable models for explainable video understanding.
