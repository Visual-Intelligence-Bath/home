Jiahui Li is a researcher focusing on Video Large Language Model (Video-LLM) understanding. Their work explores the integration of temporal dynamics and spatial semantics to enhance models' comprehension of complex visual sequences. Jiahui’s research aims to tackle core challenges such as long-video reasoning and streaming video reasoning, pushing the boundaries of how AI interprets and interacts with real-world dynamic content.
