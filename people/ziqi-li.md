Ziqi's area of research is 3D scene synthesis. Ziqi focuses on leveraging data-based methods with generative models such as diffusion and MLLM models to generate high-quality 3D scenes. For methods based on diffusion models, she plans to use MLLM-generated embeddings and encoded scene graph to help the model understand spatial relations thus designing better layouts. For methods primarily using MLLMs to generate 3D shapes and layouts, she plans to leverage the current progress in multimodal LLMs such as agents and MoE to design iterative reflective reasoning pipelines which are naturally interactable and have better capacities in planning due to the multi-step think-reflection-feedback-action loop. Her long-term goal is to develop models capable of generating high-quality 3D scenes for applications such as robotics training and artistic design directly from multimodal and iterative human prompts.
