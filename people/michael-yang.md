Michael Yang is a full Professor of Visual Intelligence at the Department of Computer Science, University of Bath. Since 2025, he is co-Director of Research Centre for Spatial Intelligence at University of Bath. His research is in the fields of Visual Intelligence and Computer Vision with specialisation on Scene Understanding, Multimodal Learning, Deep Generative Models. He published over 150 papers in international journals and conference proceedings. He serves as Editorial Board Member of International Journal of Computer Vision, and recipient of the Best Science Paper Award at BMVC 2016 and The Willem Schermerhorn Award (2021). He co-organised 15 workshops with CVPR/ICCV/ECCV.
