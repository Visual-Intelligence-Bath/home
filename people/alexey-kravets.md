Alexey's research focuses on understanding and controlling multi-modal representations, spanning four interconnected areas: (1) mechanistic interpretability of VLMs, uncovering the internal mechanisms that drive model behavior; (2) machine unlearning, developing methods to efficiently remove specific information from trained models without the cost of full retraining; (3) evaluation of VLMs, characterising the capabilities of models and methods; and (4) efficient adaptation of VLMs, enabling models to generalise to new tasks from only a handful of examples.
